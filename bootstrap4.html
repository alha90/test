Collapsible Navbar

In this example, the navigation bar is hidden on small screens and replaced by a button in the top right corner (try to re-size this window). Only when the button is clicked, the navigation bar will be displayed. Tip: You can also remove the .navbar-expand-md class to ALWAYS hide navbar links and display the toggler button.
